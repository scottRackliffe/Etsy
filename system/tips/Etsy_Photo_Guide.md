## 🎯 Why Photos Matter

Photos are the #1 factor Etsy uses to decide:

- Whether to SHOW your listing
- Whether buyers CLICK your listing
- Whether buyers TRUST your listing

Great photos = higher ranking and more sales.  
You do **not** need an expensive camera or studio.  
A simple setup + the right habits is enough.

---

# ⭐ 1. Minimal Photography Setup (What You ACTUALLY Need)

### ✔ Neutral Background

Best choices:

- Black felt/fabric → perfect for light-colored items
- White poster board → good for darker items
- Neutral gray matte paper → all-purpose

Avoid:

- Busy patterns
- Wood grain
- Wrinkled cloth
- Distracting shadows

---

### ✔ Soft, Even Lighting

The secret: **soft light, no shadows, no glare.**

Easy setup:

- Place item near a window (indirect daylight)
- Add two LED lamps at 45° angles
- If needed, diffuse light using white paper/fabric

No need for:

- Ring lights
- Fancy softboxes
- Professional studio lighting

Daylight + two lamps = perfect.

---

### ✔ iPhone Is Perfect

Modern phones outperform many DSLRs for Etsy:

- Sharp images
- Great color accuracy
- Auto-focus
- Stabilization

Use:

- HDR ON
- Portrait mode OFF
- 4:3 aspect ratio

Clean the lens with a microfiber cloth.

# ⭐ 2. The “10-Shot Recipe” — Etsy’s Ideal Photo Set

> **AiCE integration:** When AiCE generates a listing (and its shot list), the AI automatically identifies which shot type each photo is (hero, detail, backstamp, etc.) and sorts them into this recipe order. You just confirm with the dropdown on each photo — or change it if the AI got it wrong. This gives every listing a consistent, professional photo layout.

### 1️⃣ Hero Shot

- Straight on
- Shows entire item or set
- Clean, bright

### 2️⃣ Angle Shot

Soft shadows to show depth.

### 3️⃣ Detail Shot

Zoom in on:

- pattern
- texture
- transfer print
- edges

### 4️⃣ Backstamp Marking (vintage essential)

This boosts buyer trust.

### 5️⃣ Scale Shot

Include:

- ruler
- hand
- familiar object

### 6️⃣ Imperfection Shot

Document:

- crazing
- chips
- scratches
- utensil marks

Buyers appreciate honesty = fewer returns.

### 7️⃣ Underside Shot

Shows authenticity + structure.

### 8️⃣ Grouping Shot (for sets)

All pieces arranged symmetrically.

### 9️⃣ Lifestyle Shot (optional, boosts sales)

Use:

- simple kitchen table
- greenery
- clean white napkin

### 🔟 Measurement Shot

Direct ruler against the item.

# ⭐ 3. Video — The Secret Etsy Algorithm Booster

Etsy increasingly prioritizes listings with **video**.

Make a 5–10 sec clip:

- Slowly rotate item
- Or move camera side-to-side
- Silent is fine
- Keep light soft and background neutral

---

# ⭐ 4. The Perfect Etsy Photo Workflow

1. Set up background
2. Wipe item clean
3. Use soft lighting (window + LEDs)
4. Take the 10 required photos
5. Take a short video
6. Crop + straighten
7. Save images at ~3000px
8. Store in folder:

```
~/TrudysClassicTreasures/ItemPics/[SKU]/
```

# ⭐ 5. Best Practices That Improve Sales

### ✔ Brightness beats darkness

Dark photos get ignored.

### ✔ Sets should be photographed together

Shows value instantly.

### ✔ Avoid glare

Tilt item slightly or diffuse light.

### ✔ Keep backgrounds consistent

Creates shop branding.

### ✔ Always show backstamp

Collectors require authenticity.

### ✔ Photograph imperfections explicitly

Boosts trust → improves reviews → improves ranking.
