## INTRODUCTION

Etsy is not just a store; it is a search engine that acts as a matchmaker between buyers and the right listings. Most sellers fail because they simply upload photos and wait. Successful sellers use structure, clarity, categories, tags, and the search system to their advantage.

This guide teaches the method we use in Trudy’s Classic Treasures, powered by a structured listing engine.

---

# CHAPTER 1 — HOW ETSY SEARCH WORKS (BEGINNER-FRIENDLY)

Etsy Search has two steps: Matching and Ranking.

### MATCHING

Etsy looks at:

- Your title
- Tags
- Categories
- Attributes
- Description

If your listing contains the words buyers type, Etsy shows your item.

### RANKING

Once matched, Etsy sorts by:

- Relevance
- Listing quality score
- Price & shipping
- Photos
- Reviews
- Shop history

Good listings match AND rank.

---

# CHAPTER 2 — THE TITLE

The title is the most important part of your listing for search.

### What Etsy Wants:

- Clear description of what the item is
- Important words at the beginning
- No keyword stuffing
- Readable language buyers actually type

### Example:

Currier & Ives Pink Dinnerware Set – 4 Plates + 4 Bowls – Royal China Vintage

Why it's good:

- Starts with the collectible name
- Includes color and maker
- Shows quantity in simple terms

---

# CHAPTER 3 — THE CATEGORY (THE SECRET WEAPON)

Categories work like built-in tags. Choosing the correct category increases visibility dramatically.

Always select the deepest, most accurate category path.

Example for Royal China:
Home & Living → Kitchen & Dining → Dinnerware → Plates  
Home & Living → Kitchen & Dining → Dinnerware → Bowls

---

# CHAPTER 4 — ATTRIBUTES

Attributes act like extra tags. Fill out as many as possible.

For example:

- Material: Ceramic
- Color: Pink, Red
- Style: Traditional, Rustic
- Era: 1950s

More attributes = better search performance.

---

# CHAPTER 5 — TAGS (USE ALL 13!)

Tags help Etsy match your listing to shopper searches.

Rules:

- Use all 13
- Multi-word phrases
- No repeats
- Don’t duplicate attribute terms
- Think like a buyer

Example tag bank for Royal China:
currier and ives  
royal china  
pink transferware  
red transferware  
1950s dinnerware  
farmhouse decor  
cottagecore kitchen  
dinner plate set  
bowl set  
8 piece set  
americana vintage  
mid century kitchen  
vintage dinnerware

---

# CHAPTER 6 — DESCRIPTION

A good description builds trust.

Structure:

1. What it is
2. What is included
3. Condition
4. Why it’s special
5. Size & care
6. Shipping notes

Example opening:
“This charming pink Currier & Ives dinnerware set was made by Royal China between the 1950s and 1970s…”

---

# CHAPTER 7 — PRICE

Price based on:

- Real sold comps
- Condition
- Set value multiplier

Royal China Example:

- List: $98
- Accept Offer Range: $72–$78

---

# CHAPTER 8 — PHOTOS

Photos determine click-through rate.

Required shots:

- Group shot
- Individual piece shots
- Backstamp photo
- Detail close-ups
- Scale image
- Imperfection documentation

---

# CHAPTER 9 — SHIPPING

Record:

- Weight
- Dimensions
- Packaging instructions

This can feed into the EBC shipping export module when that module is implemented.
