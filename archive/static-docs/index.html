Trudy's Sales Manager

Personal inventory & sales management for vintage shops

What It Does

Trudy's Sales Manager is a personal tool built for managing a vintage and antique shop. It connects to your shop via OAuth to sync orders, manage inventory, create listing content, and generate business reports — all from a single local application.

Designed for a single shop owner, it runs on your own computer. Your data stays local in a SQLite database. Nothing is shared with third parties.

Features

1
Inventory tracking — descriptions, condition, photos, purchase costs, and listing status
2
Order management — view and manage orders synced from your shop
3
Listing creation — guided workflow to write titles, descriptions, and tags
4
Photo management — up to 20 photos per item with AI-assisted classification
5
Customer records — track buyers, addresses, and purchase history
6
Business reports — sales, costs, profit, tax summaries as PDF and CSV
7
Shipping labels — generate labels from order data and stored carrier info
8
Publish to shop — push approved listings with photos directly from the app

Technical Details

Built with Next.js and TypeScript. Runs locally on your computer. Data is stored in a local SQLite database. Connects to your shop via OAuth 2.0 with PKCE for secure authentication.

Next.js TypeScript SQLite OAuth 2.0 PKCE Tailwind CSS Local-first
